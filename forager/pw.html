Wild Chamomile


Matricaria discoidea

Also known as Pineapple Weed, this plant looks like chamomile without flower petals. The flowers are greenish-yellow and cone-shaped. Its leaves and flowers have a strong pineapple smell, and can been eaten raw and put into salads. The plant can also be used to make a fantastic tea.
