Black Locust Flowers


Robinia pseudoacacia

The black locust tree has dark-gray bark, with deep furrows. The fragrant white flowers look like pea flowers, and bunch in clusters. Only the flowers of this tree are edible; they have a sweet and floral flavor. The flowers can be eaten cooked or raw. One great way to use black locust flowers is to incorporate them in sweet or savory fritters.
