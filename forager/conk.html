Artist's Conk


Ganoderma applanatum

The Artist’s Conk is a hard, shelf-like mushroom than grows out horizontally from trees. It is known as the Artist’s Conk because the surface can be used to draw on, using a sharp object. While this mushroom is too tough to eat, is can be used to make a tea. The Artist Conk makes a fragrant tea, which is highly medicinal.
